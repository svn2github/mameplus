MAME is an emulator: it reproduces, more or less faithfully, the behaviour of\nseveral arcade machines. But hardware is useless without software, so an image\nof the ROMs which run on that hardware is required. Such ROMs, like any other\ncommercial software, are copyrighted material and it is therefore illegal to\nuse them if you don't own the original arcade machine. Needless to say, ROMs\nare not distributed together with MAME. Distribution of MAME together with ROM\nimages is a violation of copyright law and should be promptly reported to the\nauthors so that appropriate legal action can be taken.\n
